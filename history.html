History of Orlando

Orlando was founded in 1875 by a post office company called Jernigan, in a swamp. It was known for Florida's citrus industry from 1875 to 1895, until the companies in the industry shifted to a different city. The city wasn't that popular until Disney World opened in 1967, which lead to a boost in tourism, this is why it's mostly well known for theme parks. That's not all since it is the host of a tech convention called HIMSS, and has the biggest airport in Flordia.
